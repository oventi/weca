These companies do not pay fairly or are allegedly not paying fairly.
  • Minimum wage is currently $17.70 per hour.
  • Casual employees get at least a 25% extra ($22.13 per hour).
  • Maximum weekly hours are 38 hours, plus reasonable additional hours.
  • Pay slips have to be given.
